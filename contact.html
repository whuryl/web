Contact us


I am always looking for prospective Postdoc, Ph.D. or M.S. students, interns and research assistants to work with.

Applicants should have a Bachelors or Masters degree in geophysics, geology, math, computer science, physics, engineering, or a closely related field. Applicants with prior experience in computer programming, remote sensing, volcanology or seismology are particularly encouraged to apply. To introduce yourself or ask about potential opportunities, please free feel to contact me.

For info about applying to our graduate program please see http://gra.csu.edu.cn/yjsy/. If you are an international student and interested in joining our group: http://en.csu.edu.cn/Int_l_Student.htm.

Please visit the our group webpage for details. In addition to working in my group at CSU, you will also have the opportunity to interact with scientists at other organizations.
