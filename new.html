2019
  • Xiaoge and Dr. Wenbin Xu have recently published an interesting paper in JGR: Solid Earth on joint inversion of co- and post-seismic slip using LogSIM, https://doi.org/10.1029/2019JB017953.
  • Dr. Wenbin Xu has coauthored two papers: one in JGR: Solid Earth on the study of 3D displacement field of the 2016 Tottori earthquake, https://doi.org/10.1029/2018JB017159; the other is to correct the block ramp errors in optical imagery matching. doi:10.1109/ACCESS.2019.2956198.
  • Shuai Wang, one of my former RAs, has recently published two interesting papers: one in GRL on study of shallow earthquakes in Australia, https://doi.org/10.1029/2018GL080510; the other in SRL on Poso earthquake in Sulawesi, https://doi.org/10.1785/0220180211.
  • Dr. Wenbin Xu has coauthored a new paper on dynamic rupture simulation of the 2016 Kaikoura earthquake in Nature Communications, https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-019-09125-w.
  • Xiong Zhao developed an unequal weighted Laplacian smoothness constraint method for coseismic slip inversion, https://doi.org/10.1093/gji/ggz125.
